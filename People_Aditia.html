A little about me

Aditia's research interest include hydrodynamic, hydrology, high performance computing, and natural disaster hazard and risk assessment. Prior to joining Dr. Leon research group he was involved in the (re)insurance industry through utilization of hydrologic-hydraulic modeling for flood premium pricing in Indonesia. He has also outstanding experience in coastal inundation associated to sea level rise.
